## Project Management class
#### Option 1: BUS402
##### BUS402: Introduction to Project Management
https://learn.saylor.org/course/view.php?id=1147

Project management involves leading temporary efforts to achieve unique goals, distinct from the ongoing nature of operations. 
It requires a structured approach using defined process groups – initiating, planning, executing, monitoring and controlling, and closing – and draws on key competencies like strategic thinking, interpersonal communication, and business insight, as outlined by PMI's Talent Triangle. Applicable across nearly every field, project management skills are vital in today’s workplaces where employees often manage tasks alongside regular duties. 
This course introduces foundational concepts, tools, and techniques to help you effectively manage projects from start to finish.

#### Option 2: BUS605
##### BUS605: Strategic Project Management
https://learn.saylor.org/course/view.php?id=736

This is a graduate-level course. Businesses undertake projects for many reasons, such as solving business problems, creating a competitive advantage, or delivering value to the consumer. 
There are as many types of projects as there are businesses, products, processes, and operations. 
Thus, every business must decide what methods to use for various projects. Most companies will have more than one project going at the same time. The process that it uses for each project may differ. 
A software project may use an agile method, whereas product development may use a traditional method. Some projects are small and do not need a long time or financial commitments. 
Others can rise to the scope of a mega project requiring vast resources and budgets. 
Regardless of the type of project, having a definitive process for achieving the project's goals can be the difference between failure and success. 
The field of project management is so important to businesses that the PMI has developed professional standards and a certification process for people who specialize in project management and are at different stages in their careers. 
This course will follow the principles and practices of the PMI, which defines five key project process groups and ten knowledge areas. 
Ultimately, how a company undertakes and completes projects can be a competitive advantage that allows them to deliver business value.

#### Option 2: PRDV433 and PRDV430 certificates
##### PRDV433: Enhancing Spreadsheets with Generative AI
https://learn.saylor.org/course/view.php?id=1312

Welcome to Enhancing Spreadsheets with AI, a course designed to show you how generative AI tools like ChatGPT and Claude can transform how you work with spreadsheets.
In this course, you'll discover how to leverage generative AI to automate repetitive tasks, generate complex formulas, clean and transform messy data, and perform powerful data analysis using clear, natural language instructions. Whether you're building basic formulas or automating multi-step workflows, generative AI can make your Excel work faster, smarter, and more efficient.
You'll start by learning how AI fits into the modern spreadsheet ecosystem, exploring built-in AI features, and the essentials of prompt engineering to get accurate and relevant results. 
Importantly, we'll also cover the security, privacy, and ethical implications of using AI in Excel. 
As these tools often interact with sensitive business data, you'll learn best practices for responsible use, ensuring compliance with data protection regulations, protecting confidentiality, and using AI in a trustworthy and transparent manner. 
As you progress, you'll master how to use AI to generate everything from basic calculations to advanced conditional logic and learn how to easily debug and explain complex formulas. 
We'll also dive into data cleaning and analysis, where you'll use AI to reformat inconsistent entries, handle missing values, and summarize large datasets into meaningful insights and visualizations. 
Then, you'll explore how to use AI for task automation, including writing macros and VBA code, so you can automate tasks, even if you have no coding experience. 
Throughout the course, you'll get hands-on experience with popular generative AI tools, learning how to apply them to complete tasks in Excel.
By the end of this course, you'll have the skills to combine spreadsheet expertise with generative AI tools, empowering you to make data-driven decisions faster, automate smarter, and become a highly productive Excel user in the age of intelligent automation.

## Programming SQL Databases class
#### Option 1: CS403 certificate
##### CS403: Introduction to Modern Database Systems
https://learn.saylor.org/course/view.php?id=863

Though we may not recognize them in our everyday activities, databases are everywhere. 
For instance, they are fundamental to online banking profiles, airline reservation systems, medical records, and employment records. 
This course provides a general overview of databases, introducing you to database history, modern database systems, the different models used to design a database, and Structured Query Language (SQL), a pervasive language used to access and manipulate databases. 
Many of the principles of database systems are applicable to other areas of computer science, especially operating systems. 
Database systems are one of the core computer science topics since many other subjects in the discipline have been derived from this area.
